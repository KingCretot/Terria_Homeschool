--��️ Literary Device: Characterization
Definition: How an author reveals who a person is ------ through their actions, words, beliefs, and how others react to them.
"Reynolds does not present Frederick Douglass as simply a hero. He shows his brilliance, his conflict with Garrison, and the complexity of his views ------ a full human being."
💡 Why Reynolds uses this: Reynolds uses nuanced characterization to show that real people are complicated ------ even those who fight for good can still hold harmful beliefs.

📝 Grammar Focus: Active vs. Passive Voice
Active voice: the subject DOES the action. Passive voice: the subject RECEIVES the action. Passive voice can hide who is responsible.
Example: "Freedom was denied to millions." (passive ------ hides who did it) vs. "White lawmakers denied freedom to millions." (active ------ clear accountability)
