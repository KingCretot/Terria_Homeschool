--��️ Literary Device: Irony
Definition: Irony occurs when the reality of a situation is the OPPOSITE of what is expected or stated.
""Thomas Jefferson wrote that all men are created equal. Thomas Jefferson also enslaved more than 600 human beings. Both things are true.""
💡 Why Reynolds uses this: Reynolds uses irony to force you to confront uncomfortable truths ------ the gap between what Americans SAY they believe and what they actually DO.

📝 Grammar Focus: Sentence Variety
Good writers use different sentence lengths for effect. Short sentences create impact. Longer sentences build explanation and depth.
Example: "Jefferson was brilliant. Jefferson was a hypocrite. Both things are true." ------ Three short punchy sentences hit harder than one long one.
