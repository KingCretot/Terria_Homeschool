How it started

Built from a parent's own search

A Part of the Puzzle began with one mother's scramble for help — and the realization that she was not alone in it.

After being displaced by Hurricane Ian and relocating from Fort Myers to Arcadia, Florida, founder Precious Crockett went looking for resources and support for her children, including those with special needs. She was simply trying to get back to work, support her family, and find steady ground again.

The more she searched, the clearer the gap became: not only was access to resources and information scarce in the area — parents like her were exhausted. So she built the thing she had needed all along, and made Arcadia home.

The gap she found

"Parents like myself were exhausted — and the information that could help us was out of reach."
